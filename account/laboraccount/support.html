Help & Support

Find answers to common questions and get the support you need for your LaborLinko account.

How do I create a labor profile?

To create a labor profile, navigate to the sign-up page and select the "Labor" account type. Fill in your details and upload your work history or portfolio to get started.

How do I find job opportunities?

You can find job opportunities by visiting the "Find Work" section in your dashboard. Use filters to sort jobs by location, duration, and type to suit your preferences.

How do I contact employers?

You can contact employers through the in-platform messaging system. Go to the job post and click the "Message Employer" button to initiate communication.
