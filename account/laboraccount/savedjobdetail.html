Job Title: General Laborer

Job Description

We are seeking hardworking and dedicated General Laborers to assist with various physical tasks, including loading and unloading materials, cleaning work sites, and operating basic machinery. This is an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities

  • Loading and unloading materials manually or with equipment.
  • Maintaining cleanliness and safety at the work site.
  • Assisting skilled workers with tasks as needed.
  • Operating simple tools and machinery.

Requirements

  • Physical fitness and stamina to handle labor-intensive tasks.
  • Ability to follow instructions and work in a team environment.
  • Basic knowledge of tools and safety practices is a plus.
  • Prior experience in labor work is preferred but not mandatory.
